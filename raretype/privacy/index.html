Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Raretype ("Raretype", "we", "our", or "the app") is operated by DigitalCheff. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, which service providers process it, how long we keep it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, authentication credentials, and account identifier when you create an account.
  • User content and app data: trait selections, quiz result, rarity count, share profile identifier, comparisons, and app activity needed to provide features and enforce usage limits.
  • Optional information: a city you select for Pulse. Raretype does not request or store precise GPS location.
  • Optional sensitive selections: blood type and worldview or belief are included only if you explicitly opt in.
  • Purchase information: product, entitlement, and transaction identifiers needed to verify subscriptions or consumable purchases. We do not receive full payment-card details.
  • Product interaction: consent-gated, aggregated events used to understand completion and sharing performance.

2. How we use information

  • Authenticate accounts, verify email addresses, and prevent abuse.
  • Calculate entertainment estimates, save results, create share links, and compare results with friends.
  • Provide opt-in Pulse features and aggregate leaderboard entries.
  • Verify purchases, provide entitlements, and enforce free-tier limits.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve Raretype.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and deletion requests.

3. Service providers

We use infrastructure and database providers to host Raretype, an email provider for verification and password reset, Stripe for optional web payments, and Apple and RevenueCat for native purchases. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services and under their own applicable terms and privacy commitments.

Raretype does not sell personal information and does not use third-party advertising trackers.

4. Sharing and public links

A profile or comparison is available to others only when you choose to share its link. Shared links may reveal the display name and result information shown on that page. Do not share a link if you do not want others to access it.

5. Local storage and analytics

Raretype uses on-device storage for quiz progress, consent preferences, and session state. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Optional product analytics are recorded only with the applicable consent.

6. Retention

Account and saved profile data are kept until you delete your account. Expired verification codes and expired sessions are removed or invalidated. Aggregated information that no longer identifies an account may be retained to understand product performance. Payment providers may retain transaction records to meet legal and financial obligations.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Change optional consent choices in Raretype settings.
  • Delete your account and associated Raretype data in Profile → Settings → Delete account and data.
  • Clear on-device data by deleting the app.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion by emailing privacy@digitalcheff.com.

Account deletion does not cancel an Apple subscription. Apple subscriptions must be managed separately in the device's subscription settings.

8. Security and international processing

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security. Our providers may process information in countries other than your own, subject to applicable safeguards and law.

9. Children

Raretype is intended for people aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided information.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as Raretype changes. The date at the top identifies the latest version. Material changes will be communicated where required.

11. Contact

Privacy or data requests: privacy@digitalcheff.com
Product support: hello@digitalcheff.com
