Raretype Support

Need help with your account, a shared result, or a purchase? Email support@digitalcheff.com. Include the device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Do not send passwords or verification codes.

Delete your account and data

  1. Open Raretype and sign in.
  2. Open Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete account and data.
  4. Review the warning, re-authenticate if requested, and confirm deletion.

This deletes the Raretype account, active sessions, saved profile link, and associated app data that we are not legally required to retain. If you cannot access the app, email privacy@digitalcheff.com from the account email address.

Cancel an Apple subscription

Deleting a Raretype account does not cancel an Apple subscription. On iPhone or iPad, open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Raretype, then choose Cancel Subscription. Billing and refund decisions are handled by Apple.

Restore a purchase

Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase, open Raretype's purchase screen, and select Restore Purchases. Consumable reveal credits cannot be restored after use.

Response and privacy

We aim to respond to support requests within two business days. Privacy and data-rights requests can be sent to privacy@digitalcheff.com.
