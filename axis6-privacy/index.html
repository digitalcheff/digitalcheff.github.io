AXIS 6 – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

AXIS 6 ("we", "our", or "the game") is developed by DigitalCheff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play AXIS 6.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data to operate the game:

  • Game progress – scores, coins, unlocked items (stored locally on your device)
  • Device information – device model and OS version for optimization and crash tracking
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) – only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS 14+

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

AXIS 6 displays rewarded video ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

On iOS 14.5 and later, we will ask for your permission before any tracking occurs via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may deny this permission and the game will continue to function; you will still see ads but they will not be personalized.

You can change your tracking preference at any time in: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Learn more: Google Advertising & Privacy

3. Data Retention

Local game data is stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the game.

4. Children's Privacy

AXIS 6 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. EU users may also manage their ad consent preferences within the game (Settings → Privacy Policy → Manage Ad Preferences).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@digitalcheff.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
