ÇekSat Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14

Who we are

ÇekSat (“we”, “us”) is operated by DigitalCheff.

What the app does

ÇekSat helps you create marketplace listings from photos of items you want to sell.

Data we process

  • Photos you choose. Selected photos are compressed and sent to our backend for AI analysis to produce listing text.
  • Device identifier. An anonymous device ID is stored locally and used for free-trial and usage limits.
  • Purchase status. Apple / Google / RevenueCat handle payments; we receive entitlement status only.
  • Local history. Recent listings stay on your device.

AI processing

Listing generation uses OpenAI vision models via our server. Do not upload photos you are not allowed to process.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not require an account to try the free generation.

Your choices

Deny camera/photos in system settings, uninstall to clear local data, or manage subscriptions in App Store / Google Play.

Children

ÇekSat is not directed at children under 13.
